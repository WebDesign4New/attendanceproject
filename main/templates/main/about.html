ABOUT THE PROJECT

FACE RECOGNITION ATTENDANCE SYSTEM is a project which recognizes the face of the students in a real time video and automatically takes their attendace into the admin's online attendance sheet. This idea is basically implemented to help teachers to take online attendance with a proof of the students really attending their classes.


HOW TO REGISTER ?

  • 1) Students need to register themselves into the portal.
  • 2) While registering, make sure you upload a well lighted photograph of yours.
  • 3) To put the attendance, they need to login into their account first.
  • 4) Give Attendance button will appear in their profile.
  • 5) Press the button and give the attendance.
